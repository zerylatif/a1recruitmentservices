About Us To Know...

A1 Recruitment Services

We are a recruitment consultancy service that puts our customers and candidates at the core of everything we do. We thrive on building long lasting relationships with our end clients and ensure that our introduced candidates fall into a "career" and not just a "job".

How it all started

In the start of the global pandemic, Sonia Naseem, our founder, had the vision to build a next generation business to provide specialist workforce solutions. The foundation of our business always going to be underpinned by specialism, partnership and most importantly providing a great service. She embarked on a journey to build a recruitment consultancy business, from her previous employment, where she was the head of Finance, for multiple Constuction companies.

We have 20+ years' combined experience in contract and permanent solutions, with small to large construction companies around UK.

93% of our hand picked candidates remain as long lasting employees for our clients.

Our attention is focused on retention!

We are a creative, collaborative team that believe in listening first, pinpoint potential obstacles, drill down into the details and devise a strategic solution to deliver the results.

Our Mission

To pioneer workforce solutions as trusted advisors to our clients, uncover people challenges and provide effective and sustainable workforce solutions.

Investing in + Transforming the Construction Workforce industry
